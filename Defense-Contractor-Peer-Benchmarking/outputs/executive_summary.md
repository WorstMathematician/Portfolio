# Executive Summary - Defense Contractor Peer Benchmarking

## Business view

This analysis benchmarks manufacturers by estimated category revenue, market concentration, geography, and multi-year growth. It provides an external planning reference for market sizing, peer comparison, and long-range scenario assumptions.

## Questions answered

- Which companies have the largest share of the benchmark revenue pool?
- How concentrated is the peer set among the top manufacturers?
- What growth rates are reasonable for downside, base, and upside planning cases?

## Long-range planning takeaway

- Base scenario reaches 2,447.9M by 2027 using an eligible-peer median growth assumption of 4.4 percent.
- Use the peer median as a base planning anchor and the upper-quartile CAGR as an upside case, not as a forecast guarantee.
