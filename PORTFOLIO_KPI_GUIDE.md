# Portfolio KPI and Visualization Guide

The projects use Kaggle scenario datasets to demonstrate finance workflows: program control, FP&A variance analysis, executive reporting, dashboarding, peer benchmarking, and long-range planning.

## Program Finance Control Tower

- Business purpose: identify workstreams that need forecast review before a monthly business review.
- Best visuals: KPI scorecard, EAC variance status bar chart, and CPI vs. margin quadrant.
- Key indicators: Portfolio EAC Variance, Weighted CPI, Projected Margin at Completion, Watchlist Workstream Rate, Portfolio Cost Variance, Median Period Burn Rate.

## Budget vs. Actual FP&A Variance Analysis

- Business purpose: explain monthly spending performance and identify variance drivers.
- Best visuals: KPI scorecard, monthly variance threshold chart, and account variance driver chart.
- Key indicators: Net Budget Variance, Forecast Accuracy, Unfavorable Variance Exposure, Material Variance Rate, Average Monthly Absolute Variance, Largest Monthly Variance.

## Defense Contractor Peer Benchmarking

- Business purpose: size the benchmark market, assess concentration, and set external growth references for planning scenarios.
- Best visuals: KPI scorecard, market concentration chart, and long-range planning scenario line chart.
- Key indicators: Latest-Year Benchmark Revenue, Top 5 Revenue Concentration, USA Revenue Share, Eligible Median Peer CAGR, Eligible Top-Quartile CAGR, 3-Year Base LRP Growth.

## Dashboard design note

Green, Yellow, and Red status thresholds are used to make visuals action-oriented. Green means performance is within planning tolerance, Yellow means the item should be reviewed, and Red means the item needs management attention.
