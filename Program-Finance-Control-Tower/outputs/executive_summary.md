# Executive Summary - Program Finance Control Tower

## Business view

This analysis converts period-level cost accounting data into a program-control dashboard. It helps finance leaders identify which workstreams need forecast review, where cost performance is improving or deteriorating, and whether projected completion cost remains within the approved budget baseline.

## Questions answered

- Which workstreams should be discussed in the next monthly business review?
- Is projected EAC above or below the budget baseline?
- Which workstreams show margin or execution risk based on CPI, EAC variance, and risk score?
